# Top AI Trends to Watch in 2024

## Major AI Trends

**Multimodal AI Models**  
Systems that can process and generate multiple types of data: text, images, audio, and video simultaneously.

**AI Governance and Ethics**  
Growing focus on responsible AI development with emphasis on transparency and fairness.

**AI-Powered Personalization**  
Enhanced personal experiences across applications, from education to entertainment.
